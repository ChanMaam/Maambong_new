Ramen

Ramen is a Japanese noodle soup consisting of Chinese-style wheat noodles served in a meat or (occasionally) fish-based broth, often flavored with soy sauce or miso, and uses toppings such as sliced pork, nori (dried seaweed), menma (bamboo shoots), and scallions.

Dried Mango

Dried mango is a popular tropical snack made from ripe mangoes that are sliced and dried. It retains the sweet, tangy flavor of fresh mangoes but has a chewy texture, making it a convenient and tasty treat.

Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish made with vinegared rice, raw fish, and various other ingredients such as vegetables, seaweed, and sometimes tropical fruits. It is often accompanied by soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.

Pizza

Pizza is an Italian dish consisting of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients such as anchovies, mushrooms, onions, olives, vegetables, meats, etc.

Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish composed of a corn or wheat tortilla folded or rolled around a filling. A taco can be made with a variety of fillings, including beef, pork, chicken, seafood, beans, vegetables, and cheese, allowing for great versatility and variety.

Pasta

Pasta is an Italian staple food made from unleavened dough of wheat flour mixed with water or eggs, and formed into sheets or other shapes, then cooked by boiling or baking. It can be served with a variety of sauces, from tomato-based to creamy Alfredo.

Croissant

The croissant is a buttery, flaky, and layered pastry of Austrian origin, named for its crescent shape. It is made from a laminated dough, layered with butter, rolled and folded several times in succession, then rolled into a sheet, in a technique called laminating.

Cheesecake

Cheesecake is a sweet dessert consisting of one or more layers. The main, and thickest layer, consists of a mixture of soft, fresh cheese, eggs, and sugar. If there is a bottom layer, it often consists of a crust or base made from crushed cookies, graham crackers, pastry, or sometimes sponge cake.
